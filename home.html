Our Motto

R P Pansari Jan Seva Trust has been passionate about creating a modern Computer oriented Skills Centre and spread of computer and digital literacy amongst the students and more particularly the students of Government Schools at Losal where most of them are from compromising family background. The sole aim and object behind such a move was a step towards growth and development of the students and the youth who are prepared to equip themselves with the right knowledge and expertise; empower themselves and harness their skills to successfully establish themselves and thrive in their real life.

This dream is now being materialised by setting up a boutique Computech Skills Academy by the Trust with the support of and in the premises owned by Shree Gandhi Smriti Pustakalaya located in the heart of the town to make the Students and the Youth of Losal and its surrounding villages fully tech savvy and proficient in all sorts of Computer and Digital Applications and Technologies and promote e-learning in them as a habit as if it has been a part of their regular curriculum.

The Academy owes a lot to Ambuja Foundation which has been helping, assisting and guiding the Trust since a long time for this project in every respect but it could be translated to reality now only in 2025. Though it took a long time to start this, but it is well said that it's better to be late than never. Thanks to the Authorities of Ambuja Foundation once again.
