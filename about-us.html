About Our Academy

Located in the heart of Losal (Sikar), Rajasthan, Computech Skills Academy is a beacon of hope for the youth of our small town. We understand that geographical limitations shouldn't restrict access to quality education and career opportunities. Our academy, proudly operated by the RP Pansari Jan Seva Trust, is dedicated to bridging the digital divide and empowering young minds with essential 21st-century skills.

Introduction

Computech Skills Academy is a forward-thinking technical training institute dedicated to equipping students with in-demand digital skills such as Web Designing, Graphic Designing, and Digital Marketing.
Our mission is to deliver comprehensive, career-focused training that empowers students to pursue high-paying jobs, freelancing opportunities, and entrepreneurial ventures in today's competitive market.
At Computech, we are committed to providing high-quality, practical education at affordable costs, ensuring accessibility for all learners. By fostering technical proficiency and digital confidence, we aim to prepare students for lasting success in the rapidly evolving digital landscape.

Vision

At Computech Skills Academy, our vision is to create transformative opportunities that empower individuals to learn, grow, and succeed in every aspect of life.
We are dedicated to equipping every student with essential digital and technical skills, enabling them to become self-reliant, confident, and future-ready.
The Academy strives to provide training in cutting-edge, high-demand technologies while offering expert guidance to help students build successful and rewarding careers in the digital era.

Mission

  • To provide high-quality, industry-relevant education that prepares learners for real-world challenges.
  • To offer comprehensive, skill-based training that enables students to secure rewarding and competitive career opportunities.
  • To advance digital literacy and empower individuals to succeed in an increasingly technology-driven world.
  • To ensure education is both affordable and accessible, creating opportunities for learners from all backgrounds.
  • To nurture innovation, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills that drive personal and professional growth.
